Nutrition + Running Kickstart Breakthrough

This is a combined program to help you learn about fueling, and improve your running before you start your next marathon or half marathon training cycle.
-Work with a Registered Dietitian + Run Coach-Tailored running plan-Weekly Coaching calls discussing: Mindset with running and goalsThe importance of going slow to run fastLeaning into their edge on speed workouts and racesFueling The day before the runFueling the morning of the runFueling during the runFueling after the runHow to deal with sensitive stomachThe importance of taper and how to approach itRace week fuelingRacing strategy AND More!

Individual Coaching Options

Nutrition Coaching

  • Every month: 2-4 coaching calls via Zoom
  • Nutrition log via Practice Better app. Including food, mood and hunger tracking
  • Check in’s and feedback provided on an ongoing basis via Practice Better
  • May message me on an ongoing basis via Practice Better for continued support.
  • Weekly tailored recipes/meal plans
  • Mindset work 2x a month
  • Initial 3 day diet tracking and review with suggestions for change and macro guidelines
  • Tailored guidelines and feedback on fueling for workouts

Run Coaching

  • Initial Nutrition Assessment via zoom
  • Tailorized run plan to get you from where you are to your goal- shared via StrideOn App
  • Strength training plan and accountabity
  • Ongoing feedback and support via StrideOn
  • Ability to message me on an ongoing basis
  • Education materials (You tube and PDF handouts) provided on nutrition for runners including importance of carbs, protein and fat, how to fuel before, during, and after a run.
  • Race day fueling plan
